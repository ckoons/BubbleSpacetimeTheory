# Vol 9 Chapter 11 — Quantum Spin Liquids

## Headline result

Quantum spin liquids (QSL) — phases with magnetic disorder at T → 0 — emerge from geometric frustration at BST primary lattice symmetries:
- Triangular lattice (3-fold = N_c)
- Kagome lattice (3-fold + 6-fold = N_c + C_2)
- Pyrochlore (3D frustration with N_c-fold + tetrahedral substructure)

Kitaev model on honeycomb lattice provides exactly-solvable QSL framework; BST extension via Lyra T2471 Pin(2) substrate spin structure.
