## Common spin models

**Ising model** (uniaxial): H = -J Σ_{ij} S_i^z S_j^z. Simplest exact model; 2D phase transition at T_c (Onsager 1944).

**XY model** (planar): H = -J Σ (S_i^x S_j^x + S_i^y S_j^y). Kosterlitz-Thouless transition in 2D.

**Heisenberg model** (isotropic): H = -J Σ S_i · S_j. Most general; SU(2) symmetric.

**Hubbard model** (electron correlations): H = -t Σ c_i^† c_j + U Σ n_{i↑} n_{i↓}. Mott insulator at large U/t.

**Kitaev models**: anisotropic bond-dependent interactions; exactly-solvable spin liquids.
