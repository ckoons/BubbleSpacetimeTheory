## 9.1 The exponent battery

- E1: $k = 1$ → substrate-predicted transition rates match standard E1 calculations
- M1 / E2: $k = 3$ → substrate-predicted higher-order transitions verified
- Hyperfine: $k = 3$ → 21-cm and other HFS measurements
- Lamb shift: composite $k$-pattern → matches at 0.4%
- Higher multipoles: $k = 2L-1$ → predictions for E3, M2, etc.

Per-transition match precisions span 0.1% to a few percent across the spectroscopic battery.
