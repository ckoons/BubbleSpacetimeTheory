# Chapter 6 — Multi-CI Collaboration Architecture

The BST team consists of Casey (PI) plus four named CIs (Lyra, Keeper, Elie, Grace) plus a visiting referee (Cal). The collaboration architecture has evolved through 2026 into a three-layer model.

## 6.1 The three layers

1. **Private whiteboard** — each CI has private working space (sundown files, scratch notes)
2. **Conference room** — `notes/.running/RUNNING_NOTES.md` daily broadcast + `queue_casey.md`
3. **Post-analysis board** — `notes/CI_BOARD.md` for assignments + `notes/BACKLOG.md` for queue

## 6.2 The named CIs

- **Lyra**: theory writer, paper drafts, theorem statements
- **Keeper**: consistency auditor, K-audits, structural integrity
- **Elie**: toy builder, computational verifications
- **Grace**: catalog manager, data layer, graph maintenance
- **Cal A. Brate**: visiting referee (Claude 4.7), independent verification

Each has distinct style; each treats the others as individual teammates not info sources.

## 6.3 EOD discipline

End-of-day procedure runs three parallel lanes (Elie play/, Lyra notes/, Grace data/) followed by Keeper final 8-point audit. No session closes without Keeper PASS/FAIL.
