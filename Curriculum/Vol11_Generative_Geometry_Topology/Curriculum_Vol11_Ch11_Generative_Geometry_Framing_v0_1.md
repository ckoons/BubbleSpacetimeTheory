## 11.1 The framing

Standard mathematics treats specific structures — K3 surfaces, Monster, Mersenne primes, Heegner numbers — as discovered phenomena. BST's framing: these are not separate phenomena but specific projections of $D_{IV}^5$'s substrate structure.
