## RIGOROUSLY CLOSED tier requirements (detailed)

### Requirement 1: Alt-HSD comparison

Explicit comparison against at least 2 alternative HSDs at the criterion's structural level. The alternatives must be tested for the same property and FAIL to match.

**T2439 example**: D_IV⁵ tested against D_I_{1,5} + D_I_{5,1} (two alternative HSDs at dim_C = 5). Both alternatives have lowest Casimir = 4; only D_IV⁵ has lowest Casimir = 6.

### Requirement 2: EXACT-match in BST primary form

The criterion's positive result on D_IV⁵ must be EXACT match (algebraic identity) to a BST primary integer or BST-primary-derived expression. Not approximate match; not experimental precision match — algebraic identity.

**T2439 example**: D_IV⁵ lowest Casimir C_2 = (1+5/2)² + (3/2)² − 17/2 = 6 = T_{N_c} (BST primary, color singlet triangle T1930). EXACT algebraic identity.

### Requirement 3: If-and-only-if distinguishability

The criterion must be both NECESSARY and SUFFICIENT for D_IV⁵. Alternative HSDs satisfying the property would falsify the if-and-only-if; alternative HSDs failing the property must FAIL distinctly (not near-miss).

**T2439 example**: D_IV⁵ ⇔ lowest Casimir = 6. D_I_{1,5} has Casimir = 4 (distinct, not near-miss). D_I_{5,1} has Casimir = 4. If-and-only-if structure verified.

### Requirement 4: Mathematical theorem-level rigor

The criterion's substrate-derivation must be theorem-grade — formal proof with citations, not just structural identification. Lyra T-number registry + Cal dual-axis paper-grade PASS required.

**T2439 example**: T2439 registered theorem; Lyra Toy 3236 D_IV⁵ explicit C8 closure; Cal #77 ACCEPTED.
