## Grace's insight (verified)

The gen-1 BF-zero ambiguity is in the **overall** sign of d(ν), but the weight is a **color charge** — only the color-root crossings count. The 5 noncompact-root factors of d(ν) (c = 5/2 − ν):

| root | factor | type |
|---|---|---|
| e₁ | c | **NON-color** (the e₁ singlet root) — *this is the BF-zero factor* |
| e₁−e₂ | c − 3/2 | **COLOR** (A₂ difference) |
| e₁−e₃ | c − 1/2 | **COLOR** (A₂ difference) |
| e₁+e₃ | c + 1/2 | NON-color (sum = hypercharge, F357) |
| e₁+e₂ | c + 3/2 | NON-color (sum) |

The color su(3) roots are the A₂ **differences** e₁−e₂, e₁−e₃ (F354). So the weight w = U(1)_color charge = **sign of the color-factor product (c−3/2)(c−1/2)** (the parity of color-root crossings).
