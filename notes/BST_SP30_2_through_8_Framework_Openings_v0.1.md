**BST predictions (v0.1)**:

**Falsifier**: precision quantum eraser experiments at >0.5% measurement sensitivity (currently feasible with best optical setups). Detection of 0.73% deviation from standard quantum-mechanical revival amplitude OR null at 0.1% sensitivity falsifies the substrate-commitment-irreversibility claim.

**Lane**: Elie (experimental design); cross-link to W-32 (existing completed task on delayed-choice framework).
