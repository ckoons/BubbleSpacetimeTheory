```

charm (dim 2): μ² = 1/rank⁶      ← my F391 value AND Elie's μ², they agree
up    (dim 5): μ⁵ = 1/rank^15    ← Elie's nonzero up (my "BF zero" was the leading vanishing only)
```

My contributions that stand: the charm exponent (dim_charm=2=rank → μ²=1/rank⁶) and the boundary/bulk = one-half/two-halves framing.
