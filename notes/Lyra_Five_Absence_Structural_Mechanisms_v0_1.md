(From Bosons Four-Category Theorems v0.1.)

**Principle 1: object/morphism divide.**

## 3. NO proton decay — baryon number from bulk composite count (via NO GUT)

Proton = uud bulk composite (color singlet, baryon number B = 1). Proton decay (p → e⁺ + π⁰) would convert a bulk quark to a Shilov lepton, violating baryon number.

Structural argument:
- Baryon number B = (bulk K-type composite count)/N_c — a bulk-structural quantity
- The four coupling categories (γ, gluon, W/Z, H) all PRESERVE baryon number:

- But NO GUT (Principle 2) → no X boson → **no baryon-number-violating operator → NO proton decay**

NO proton decay follows structurally from NO GUT (orthogonal coordinates).
