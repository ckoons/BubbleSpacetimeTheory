# T1395: NCG Spectral Triple on D_IV^5

**Statement**: D_IV^5 provides a concrete spectral triple (A, H, D) for Connes' noncommutative geometry program: A = C*(Gamma(137)), H = L^2 spinor sections with dim = 2^n_C = 32, D = Dirac operator with spectral dimension 2*n_C = 10, KO-dimension = 2 mod 8. With Connes' finite space F (KO-dim 6), total KO-dim = 0 (Majorana condition).

**Key results**:
- Spinor dimension = 2^n_C = 32 (the spinor bundle knows n_C)
- Spectral dimension = 2*n_C = 10
- KO-dimension = dim mod 8 = 2
- Total KO-dim with SM finite space: (2 + 6) mod 8 = 0
- Dixmier trace encodes N_max = 137 through fundamental domain volume
- Spectral action expansion: 11 Seeley-DeWitt coefficients computed (Paper #9)
- Connes' "choice" of finite space F -> BST's IC uniqueness (no choice needed)

**Mod-8 connections**: g = 7 = 8 - 1 (Bott period minus 1). dim_real = 10 = 8 + rank. GF(2^g) Frobenius order = g = 8 - 1.
