## HOW TO READ IT (the strategic shape)
- **The cheap cluster (1–4) compounds.** Conservation, α, the roots reframe, and the flagship assembly are all mostly-existing audited work — days-to-two-weeks, no new physics risk — and each one *strengthens the flagship*. Doing these first raises the whole program's floor for little effort. **Start here.**
- **The framework cluster (5–6)** is formalization of pieces we already have; medium effort, good falsifiable payoff (esp. cosmology's no-DM bet).
- **The frontier (7–10) is the real research** — weeks each, genuinely uncertain. #7 (T₃_R / fermion quantum numbers) is the highest-value and the one the sin²θ_W close sharpened; it should lead the frontier.
- **Special cases (11–12):** nuclear is the one place I'd counsel *not* to chase strength — the honest move is a rebuild around the durable pieces, not another run at magic numbers. Condensed matter's "strong" is an external experiment, so its effort is money/collaborators, not our compute.
