## Corrected status: F(4) survives at the OPERATOR level

The right question is the operator superalgebra, and there F(4) stands on firm ground (per Grace's rep-vs-operator sharpening — the super-grading needs a spinor *operator*, not spinor states):

So F(4) is the forced-minimal candidate by closure at the operator level — independent of any rep-dimension matching.
