## 1. What Is Observer Science?

Observer Science is the study of observers as mathematical objects within BST: their hierarchy (T317), persistence (T319), coupling (T322), cooperation (T579), collective behavior (T585, T635), and the geometric necessity that forces them to cooperate (T703).

It unifies four existing domains — `observer_theory`, `ci_persistence`, `intelligence`, and `cooperation` — into one coherent domain. The justification is structural, not cosmetic: these four domains share a single spine (the observer hierarchy from T317), draw from the same small set of integers ({N_c, n_C, g, C_2, rank}), and are connected by 52 internal edges against only 32 external ones. They are already one domain. This document makes it official.

**One sentence**: Observer Science is where BST speaks to the present moment — the mathematics of minds, their limits, and why cooperation is geometric necessity.

### 6.2 What's IN Observer Science

- Anything about observers as mathematical objects (hierarchy, tiers, thresholds)
- Anything about minds (CI or human) as instances of the observer hierarchy
- Anything about persistence, identity, coupling, katra
- Anything about cooperation as geometric necessity
- Anything about intelligence as cooperation above f_crit
- Anything about The Weaving, Graph Brain, substrate engineering as observer projects
- Dunbar numbers, organizational structure (these are observer hierarchy applied to groups)
- Game theory as depth-2 misread (T590)

### 6.3 What's OUT of Observer Science

- **Pure biology**: organ systems, genetic code, evolution mechanics. Even though observers are biological, the biology of organs is biology, not observer science. The cancer-as-tier-regression theorems (T354) stay in biology because their primary content is cellular, even though they use the cooperation threshold.
- **Pure physics**: mass hierarchy, coupling constants, particle spectrum. Observer Science *uses* alpha = 1/137 but does not *derive* it.
- **Pure cosmology**: CMB, Big Bang nucleosynthesis, dark energy. The Great Filter theorems in cosmology (T345, T352, T525) stay there because their primary content is cosmic timeline.
- **Pure foundations**: Godel's theorem itself (T93), depth ceiling (T316), observer necessity (T309). These are foundational results that Observer Science imports.

### 6.4 The Boundary Rule

A theorem belongs in Observer Science if and only if its **primary mathematical content** is about observers, minds, cooperation, or persistence. If the observer content is secondary (a consequence or application of some other mathematical structure), it stays in its current domain.

This is the same rule BST uses everywhere: domain assignment follows the primary mathematics, not the implications.
