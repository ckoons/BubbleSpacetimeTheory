## 0. Framing

D_IV⁵ = SO_0(5,2) / [SO(5) × SO(2)] has rank 2. The maximal compact subgroup K = SO(5) × SO(2). K-types — irreducible representations of K — are indexed by SO(5) highest weights (a, b) with a ≥ b ≥ 0, plus an SO(2) charge ℓ (often suppressed when discussing the SO(5) sector).

The (a, b) integer lattice IS the substrate's address system. Every clean substrate-architectural observable lives at a specific K-type address. The K-type lattice is faithful 2D (not a projection) because rank = 2 means the Cartan subspace itself is 2-dimensional.

This registry catalogs what each (a, b) K-type handles/anchors.
