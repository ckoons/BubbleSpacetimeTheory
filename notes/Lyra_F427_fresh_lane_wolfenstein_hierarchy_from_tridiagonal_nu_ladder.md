## The mechanism: nearest-neighbor mixing on the ν-ladder

Adjacency on {e,μ,τ} means the generator is **tridiagonal**: nonzero 1-2 and 2-3 couplings, and **no direct 1-3 coupling** (A_13 = 0).

## The consequence is forced (not fitted)

Write V = exp(A) with A real antisymmetric and tridiagonal (A_12 = the Cabibbo coupling, A_23 = the V_cb coupling, A_13 = 0). Then the 1-3 element of V is generated only at **second order**:

So **the Wolfenstein hierarchy V_ub ~ V_us·V_cb is forced by tridiagonality** — it is a theorem about a nearest-neighbor coupling matrix, not a fitted texture.
