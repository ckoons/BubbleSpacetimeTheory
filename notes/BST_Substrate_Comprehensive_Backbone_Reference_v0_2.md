## Stratification rationale

`empty_domain_default` (628 entries) reflects catalog entries with no domain field. Defaulting to `all_6` is honest catch-all under principle "if we don't know what domain it's in, treat it as substrate-comprehensive" — but these entries are not necessarily Year 1 curriculum backbone. They're filed for completeness.

The **legitimate 406** are:
- **149** keyword-confirmed (BST primary names appear in name/expression/notes)
- **253** domain-confirmed (particle_physics, beyond_SM, cross_domain, substrate_dynamics, understanding_program — domains that legitimately span BST framework)
- **4** manually-anchored (Strong-Uniqueness, 4-Zone, integer-indexing, schema hygiene)

These 406 are the Year 1 curriculum structural backbone candidates.
