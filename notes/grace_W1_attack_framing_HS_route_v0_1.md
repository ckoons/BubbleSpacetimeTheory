# W1 — the constructive core, framed via the HS route

**W1 = construct 4D Yang-Mills as a rigorous (Wightman/Osterwalder-Schrader) QFT and prove the gap.** This is open
**in mathematics itself** — no interacting 4D QFT has ever been constructed. The HS route does **not** solve it.
What it does is *relocate* the constructive burden to where BST has exact tools, and name the residual precisely.
This note is the attack framing; W1 stays open.

## The three ingredients, by where their rigor stands

1. **The bulk D_IV⁵ spectral theory** = harmonic analysis on a bounded symmetric domain. **Rigorous and
   classical** (Hua-Korányi, Faraut-Korányi): the Hardy space H², the Casimir spectrum, the Szegő kernel — all
   textbook, not an open construction. The gap = C₂ is an exact eigenvalue.
2. **HS (T2489)** = exact unitary interior ↔ Shilov boundary. Transfers the bulk data **exactly**: Hilbert space,
   Hamiltonian (Casimir), spectrum, and — the L3 lead — Szegő-kernel positivity = **OS reflection positivity**. So
   the boundary inherits a rigorous positive-energy, reflection-positive structure from the bulk, for free.
3. **The boundary object** = 4D conformal YM (UV fixed point). The **identification "this boundary theory IS YM"**
   is the load-bearing step.

## The relocation (the useful structural point)

- **Direct W1:** construct interacting YM on flat R⁴ — the famous open-in-mathematics problem.
- **HS-routed W1:** (a) the bulk is rigorous BSD harmonic analysis **[have it]** + (b) HS transfers it exactly
  **[have it, T2489]** + (c) identify the transferred boundary theory as YM.

So the constructive rigor lives on the **bulk** side (tractable), and the interaction is encoded in the bulk
**curvature** (Casimir gap; Bergman curvature −2/g) — the standard holographic "classical bulk ↔ interacting
boundary." This is why R⁴ "can't host the scale" (the no-go) but the bulk can: the bulk *is* where the scale and
the dynamics live.

## What W1 reduces to (honestly)

W1 → **"is the holographic map (rigorous BSD bulk) → (boundary 4D QFT) itself rigorous, and is the image 4D YM?"**
— the BST-specific version of *making AdS/CFT rigorous*. Two halves:

- **The identification-as-YM half** = the W2 spectrum match run *to completeness* (the dynamics/correlators, not
  just the gap) — the same computation the team is closing.
- **The rigorous-holographic-map half** = the genuine residual. It is *more tractable here than generic AdS/CFT*
  because: the bulk is a BSD with **exact** harmonic analysis (not a generic AdS), HS is an **exact** isometry (not
  an asymptotic dictionary), and OS-positivity is **already in hand** (positive Szegő kernel).

## ⚠️ The sharp caveat (the real edge — flagged, not buried)

**The relocation risks delivering rigor for the WRONG theory.** In AdS/CFT, a *free* bulk field gives a
**generalized-free** boundary theory — rigorous, but *not* an interacting CFT. The D_IV⁵ "spectral theory"
(harmonic analysis) is free-field-*like* (a discrete Casimir spectrum). So the live danger is: **HS transfers the
bulk exactly, but the boundary you get is generalized-free, not interacting Yang-Mills.** For the image to be YM,
the interaction must genuinely be encoded — in the bulk curvature / the non-trivial K-type structure / bulk
vertices — and *proving the boundary correlators are interacting (not generalized-free)* is the heart of the
residual. **This is exactly where the W2 spectrum match to completeness becomes load-bearing for W1:** a full
spectrum with the right *interacting* glueball structure (anomalous dimensions, correct correlator ratios) is the
evidence the boundary isn't generalized-free. So W1's hard half and W2's completeness are the same question seen
from the constructive side.
