All nine Millennium-class problems sit at depth ≤ 2.

That **is** the Mirror Transfer in complexity language:

| Mirror Transfer (F230/F231/T2489) | Pair Resolution / depth-2 (T316) |

So the Mirror Transfer is the **geometry** and the Pair Resolution is the **complexity** of one fact: rank 2.
