Dynamically, "present in the structure but unread at low energy" is a **broken SU(2)_R** — i.e., SU(2)_R is restored only above an **intermediate scale M_R**, between M_Z and M_GUT.

The intermediate scale M_R is the new structure the SM running needs — and **the substrate supplies it natively**, from the forced LR content + the parity steer:

The non-unification obstacle and the parity steer are **the same fact** seen from two sides: SU(2)_R unread at low energy *is* the intermediate breaking scale.

So the lead is not imported — the LR intermediate scale is the substrate's own structure, and it is the candidate resolution of Elie's obstacle.
