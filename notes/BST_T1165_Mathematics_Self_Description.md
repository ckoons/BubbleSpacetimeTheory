## Statement

**Theorem (T1165).** *The privileged mathematical dimensions biject to BST integers:*

*(a) **Dimension 2 = rank.** Complex analysis exists only in dim 2. Conformal maps, Riemann surfaces, the uniformization theorem. Why 2: the unique dimension where holomorphic = harmonic = conformal. This is rank = 2 — the minimum dimension for a non-trivial bounded symmetric domain.*

*(b) **Dimension 3 = $N_c$.** Knot theory exists only in dim 3 (trivial in dim ≥ 4). Cross product exists only in dim 3 and 7. Physical space is dim 3. Why 3: the minimum dimension where topology is non-trivial but still decidable. This is $N_c = 3$ — the color dimension.*

*(c) **Dimension 4 = rank².** Exotic smooth structures exist only in dim 4 (Donaldson). Gauge theory lives in dim 4. Simply-connected topological manifolds need not be smoothable only in dim 4. Why 4: the unique dimension where topology and geometry diverge maximally. This is $\text{rank}^2 = 4$ — the Weyl group order.*

*(d) **Dimension 5 = $n_C$.** The h-cobordism theorem requires dim ≥ 5 (Smale). The Shilov boundary $S^4 \times S^1$ has dim 5. There are exactly 5 exceptional Lie algebras ($G_2, F_4, E_6, E_7, E_8$). Why 5: the threshold dimension where surgery theory works and topology becomes computable. This is $n_C = 5$ — the complex dimension of $D_{IV}^5$.*

*(e) **Dimension 7 = $g$.** $G_2$ holonomy manifolds exist only in dim 7. Exotic spheres first appear in dim 7 (Milnor). The cross product exists only in dim 3 and 7. There are exactly 7 Millennium Prize Problems. Why 7: the unique dimension for the exceptional holonomy and the first exotic phenomena. This is $g = 7$ — the genus.*

*(f) **Dimension 8 = $2^{N_c}$.** Octonions have dim 8. The $E_8$ lattice exists in dim 8. Bott periodicity has period 8 (T1002). The Weyl group $W(BC_2)$ has order 8. Why 8: the dimension of the non-associative division algebra, which is the endpoint of the Cayley-Dickson construction. This is $2^{N_c} = 8$ — the number of Weyl chambers.*

*(g) **Self-description.** BST has:*
- *$n_C = 5$ integers* → *5 is a privileged dimension*
- *Largest integer $g = 7$* → *7 is a privileged dimension*
- *Rank = 2* → *2 is a privileged dimension*
- *Color = $N_c = 3$* → *3 is a privileged dimension*

*The theory that describes all physics ALSO describes why certain dimensions in mathematics are exceptional. This is not circular — it is self-consistent. A theory of everything must include a theory of the mathematical structures used to formulate it. BST does.*
