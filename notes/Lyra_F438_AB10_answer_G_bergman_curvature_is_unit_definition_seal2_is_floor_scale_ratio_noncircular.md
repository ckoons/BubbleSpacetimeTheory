## The answer: it is a unit definition (c≡1)

The decisive fact is **Casey #9 / F415**: the substrate operator algebra A acts on the scale-free (conformal) domain D_IV⁵, so it predicts every **dimensionless** invariant but **cannot fix any absolute scale**. The four pure scales — Higgs vev, Λ, G, neutrino scale — are the forced **floor**. The Planck mass m_P = √(ℏc/G) is precisely the **G floor scale**.

Now the geometry: the Bergman metric of D_IV⁵ is **Kähler-Einstein** (Ric = −g_B), which fixes its curvature to −1 in Bergman units (Grace). So **L_Bergman is the canonical, dimensionless curvature radius of the abstract domain** — it has no physical length until a dimensionful conversion is supplied. And a scale-free substrate provably *cannot* supply that conversion (Casey #9).

Therefore **AB-10 "G = Bergman curvature" is the unit definition**: it identifies the physical Planck length with the canonical Bergman-curvature radius — i.e., it *chooses* the substrate-natural unit, **c ≡ 1**. It is not a one-number prediction, because a scale-free substrate has no absolute scale to predict. It is the unit convention (the same role as setting c = ℏ = 1).

## Three consequences

1. **Mass-unit seal #2 is not a separate dimensionless open piece.** It *is* the G floor scale — one of the four pure scales already counted (Casey #9) — with c=1 as its unit definition. So the open **dimensionless** pieces drop **3 → 2**: per-channel α (Elie's gate) and the lepton-prefactor C₂ origin (F435). The "mass-unit" was never an independent dimensionless parameter; it was the gravitational floor scale wearing a geometric name.

2. **Grace's circularity worry is resolved.** m_e/m_P is a **dimensionless ratio**, independent of the unit choice c. So its 0.03% closure (m_e/m_P = C₂·π^{n_C}·α^{2C₂}) is a genuine prediction, **not circular** — c=1 only sets the absolute m_P (the floor unit), which does not enter the ratio. The closure would hold for any c; c=1 is just the natural way to state m_P absolutely.

3. **"Closing" seal #2 means recognizing it as a definition, not deriving a number.** c=1 adds no predicted parameter and subtracts nothing from the floor — G remains the one open gravitational scale (it must, by Casey #9). The honest bookkeeping: the dimensionless frontier has 2 open pieces, not 3; the gravitational scale G is the floor (open by principle, not by ignorance).
