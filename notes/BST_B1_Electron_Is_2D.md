## The Claim

$$\dim \mathcal{H}_{e^-}^{\mathrm{intrinsic}} \;=\; \mathrm{rank}(D_{IV}^5) \;=\; 2$$

**The electron is rank-2, not 4-dimensional. Nothing is hidden.**
