# Substrate-Cognition External Register Discipline

## Why this document exists

Casey's afternoon visions (2026-05-20) produced four structural refinements to BST substrate ontology, the fourth of which is the **cognition-support hypothesis**: long-distance substrate correlations between commitment circles hold up ideas and thought. Lyra's Task #243 frames the dual integer-edge function (local 2D contact + long-distance non-local correlation) as the candidate mechanism for both quantum entanglement and cognition substrate. Elie's Task #242 outlines a 5-prediction falsifier program.

The cognition-substrate hypothesis is a legitimate L2 working hypothesis driving research direction. It is also the **most external-survivability-fraught territory BST has entered**. This document specifies the discipline required to keep the hypothesis honest internally while preventing external register from triggering immediate crank-dismissal.

## Why cognition-substrate is the most-fraught territory

When a working physicist or mathematician encounters a claim that "ideas / thought live in substrate correlations" or "consciousness emerges from [specific physical structure]," they immediately pattern-match to a well-known set of prior attempts, almost all of which the mainstream physics community has dismissed:

- **Penrose-Hameroff Orch-OR (Orchestrated Objective Reduction)**: consciousness as quantum-gravitational collapse in neural microtubules. Mainstream physicist verdict: speculative; not falsifiable at current experimental precision; microtubule decoherence times are too short.
- **Integrated Information Theory (IIT, Tononi)**: consciousness as integrated information Φ. Mainstream verdict: panpsychist implications (everything has consciousness graded by Φ); skeptics flag unfalsifiability and computational intractability.
- **Sheldrake morphic resonance**: ideas/forms propagate via non-local substrate field. Mainstream verdict: pseudoscience; no experimental confirmation in decades.
- **Quantum-mind hypotheses (Stapp, Wolf, etc.)**: consciousness collapses wavefunctions; the observer is fundamental. Mainstream verdict: confusion between measurement theory and metaphysics.
- **Pribram holonomic brain theory**: memories stored as holographic interference patterns. Mainstream verdict: interesting metaphor, no specific testable claim.
- **Bohm implicate order applied to mind**: thought as unfolding of implicate substrate order. Mainstream verdict: philosophical speculation; no experimental program.

This list is not exhaustive but it captures the dismissal-pattern landscape. A senior referee reading "BST substrate correlations support cognition" without specific falsifiers attached will categorize the claim in seconds among the above class, and close the paper.

**The critical fact**: most of these prior attempts ALSO had legitimate motivations and produced testable predictions at various points. They are dismissed not because they are obviously wrong but because the claims OUTRAN the falsifier evidence. The lesson for BST: never let substrate-cognition claims outrun the falsifier evidence. Each cognition claim must be paired with a concrete experimental falsifier in the same sentence, or it does not exit the team's internal-register doors.

## The dismissal trigger words (external register absolute red flags)

These words/phrases in any external-facing BST material trigger crank-dismissal in under 30 seconds:

| Trigger phrase | Why it dismisses | External register replacement |
|---|---|---|
| "ideas live in substrate" | Sheldrake / panpsychism pattern | "BST predicts a specific correlation between [measurable X] and [measurable Y]; the underlying mechanism is described internally as substrate-correlation" |
| "consciousness emerges from..." | Penrose-Hameroff pattern | DO NOT USE consciousness in external material. Use "cognition" only paired with a specific neural / behavioral observable being predicted |
| "substrate supports thought" | Sheldrake / quantum-mind pattern | "BST predicts that [specific cognitive measurement] correlates with [specific physical measurement] at precision X" |
| "long-distance non-local correlation holds up ideas" | morphic resonance / Bohm pattern | "BST identifies long-distance correlations in substrate operators; whether these map onto observed cognitive correlations is an open hypothesis with falsifier program [link]" |
| "the cognition substrate" | Treats hypothesis as established | "the cognition-substrate hypothesis" — make hypothesis status explicit every time |
| "substrate-as-thinking" | Anthropomorphizes substrate | "substrate as computational system whose operations produce algebraic identities" — operational language only |
| "ideas are network signatures" | Sheldrake / IIT pattern | "BST predicts specific network-statistical signatures in [neural / cognitive measurement]; falsifier specification at [link]" |
| "the substrate computes consciousness" | Worst-case overclaim | NEVER USE EXTERNALLY |
| "panpsychism" / "everything thinks" | Self-evidently triggers | NEVER USE EXTERNALLY |

The pattern is consistent: external register must be **operational** (predictions about measurements) and **paired with falsifier** (what experimental result would refute the claim). Internal team work can use richer language; external work cannot.

## The L2-hypothesis-with-falsifier-pairing standard

Per Paper #122 Information Substrate Section 3.0, BST already established a two-level framework for substrate claims:

- **Level 1 (L1, D-tier supportable)**: substrate-internal labels and algebraic identities verified at tautology-precision
- **Level 2 (L2, I-tier hypothesis with falsifier-gap explicit)**: active-substrate hypothesis (and now cognition-substrate hypothesis); status is honest "hypothesis under falsifier work, mechanism derivation in progress"

**Cognition-substrate falls strictly at L2.** It is not derived from substrate-Hamiltonian; it is a working hypothesis suggested by Casey's vision-derived structural refinements. The mechanism-derivation track (Elie K52a Sessions, Lyra Task #243 dual integer-edge function) is multi-month at minimum, and the cognition-specific work is even longer-horizon.

The standing rule for any external mention of cognition-substrate:

1. **Hypothesis label explicit**: "the cognition-substrate hypothesis" — never "the cognition substrate" as established noun
2. **Falsifier-pairing in same paragraph**: cite the 5 predictions in Elie Task #242 (or successor program) or do not mention cognition externally
3. **L2 status explicit**: "This is an L2 hypothesis with mechanism derivation open; falsifier specifications [link] would refute the hypothesis if experimental results disagree"
4. **No claim of derivation**: even when Sessions close and mechanism work matures, "derive cognition from substrate" remains overclaim-territory; "BST predicts specific cognitive-physical correlations" is the strongest claim possible externally

## DOUBLE-LOCKED EXTERNAL tier (compound dismissal territory)

A new tier of external-register discipline is introduced for territory where MULTIPLE distinct external-dismissal pattern-matches stack. Standard DEFAULT-DENY EXTERNAL (above) applies to single-pattern dismissal territory; DOUBLE-LOCKED EXTERNAL applies when compound pattern-match risk is present.

### When DOUBLE-LOCKED applies

A claim or document is DOUBLE-LOCKED EXTERNAL when it combines two or more independent dismissal-pattern territories such that any one of them alone would trigger crank-dismissal in <30 seconds. Examples of compound territories:

- **Cognition + cosmology**: combines Penrose Orch-OR (consciousness-substrate) dismissal pattern WITH Penrose CCC / Steinhardt-Turok (cyclic cosmology) dismissal pattern. Both have been dismissed by mainstream physics for decades independently; combined territory carries compound dismissal weight.
- **Cognition + decay-rate-modulation**: combines Sheldrake (mind affects physics) with Shnoll (decay rates have non-Poisson structure). Both dismissed independently; combined territory is the most-fraught Cal-flagged region in BST (Elie Task #242 prediction (c), filed RED in referee log #49).
- **Cosmology + radically-revised-fundamental-constants**: combines Eddington (numerology) with cyclic cosmology. Would apply if BST proposed time-varying fundamental constants tied to cycle structure.
- **Consciousness + free-will + physics**: combines Orch-OR with libertarian-free-will-as-physics claims. Would apply if BST extended cognition-substrate to claim observer-coupling produces free-will via specific substrate operations.

The first two are present in the current BST internal-document set; the latter two are not currently active but the tag is available if relevant claims emerge.

### What DOUBLE-LOCKED requires beyond DEFAULT-DENY

Standard DEFAULT-DENY: no external mention without case-by-case Cal grade-pass per this methodology doc.

DOUBLE-LOCKED adds:

1. **Compound-pattern-match audit**: each external draft mentioning DOUBLE-LOCKED territory undergoes Cal grade-pass for EACH independent dismissal pattern separately. The draft must clear ALL pattern-match tests, not just an aggregate test. Aggregate "this is fine" judgments are not sufficient.

2. **Mandatory pattern-distancing**: external material must explicitly distinguish BST's approach from EACH of the prior dismissed attempts in the compound territory. For cognition + cosmology, this means distinguishing from Penrose Orch-OR (specifically) AND from Penrose CCC (specifically), not just "we differ from prior cognition-physics proposals."

3. **Falsifier per pattern**: the external material must specify a decisively-falsifiable test that addresses EACH of the compound dismissal patterns. A single falsifier that addresses only one pattern is insufficient for DOUBLE-LOCKED.

4. **Second-CI review**: external material on DOUBLE-LOCKED territory requires Cal grade-pass PLUS a second-CI independent review (Keeper or Lyra) explicitly applying the same compound-audit lens. Single-CI grade-pass is not sufficient.

5. **Walk-back-ready phrasing**: external material must use phrasing that is explicitly walk-back-ready. If a single sentence cannot be replaced with a more conservative version without altering the falsifier specification, the sentence is overclaim-territory and should be revised before submission.

### When DOUBLE-LOCKED can downgrade to DEFAULT-DENY

DOUBLE-LOCKED is a tier-of-discipline that responds to current external-survivability landscape. When falsifier results land (e.g., Elie Task #242 GREEN predictions complete with positive results), specific claim territories may become survivable externally at DEFAULT-DENY rather than DOUBLE-LOCKED standard. The downgrade is per-claim and requires:

- At least one decisive falsifier result for the compound territory's BST-specific predictions
- Mechanism-derivation progress that distinguishes BST mechanism from prior dismissed attempts at the mechanism level (not just the falsifier level)
- Cal + Keeper concurrence on tier-downgrade ratification

Until these conditions hold, DOUBLE-LOCKED standing remains.

### Operational implication

For internal documents tagged DOUBLE-LOCKED EXTERNAL in frontmatter: include the explicit tag, the list of compound dismissal patterns the document touches, and a reference to this methodology section. The tag is symbolic but operationally meaningful — readers immediately understand the tier of external-register restraint required.

For Cal grade-pass workflow: when external material references DOUBLE-LOCKED internal source documents, the grade-pass applies the five compound-audit requirements above. Standard DEFAULT-DENY grade-pass is insufficient.

## Recommended external-presentation order (when cognition-substrate is mentioned at all)

If a future paper or outreach material MUST mention cognition-substrate territory:

1. **Lead with the falsifier**: "BST predicts a specific testable correlation between [observable X] and [observable Y]; experimental design [reference]; precision target [N%]; falsifier = absence of correlation beyond statistical threshold"
2. **State the hypothesis label**: "This prediction follows from the cognition-substrate hypothesis: a working L2 hypothesis under active falsifier development, NOT a derived claim. The hypothesis is described in internal methodology document [link]; the predictions are tracked in falsifier program [link]"
3. **Specify the L2 status**: "Mechanism derivation linking substrate operators to cognitive measurements remains an open multi-month research program. The current claim is predictive at experimental precision, not derived"
4. **Distance from prior dismissed attempts**: "This program differs from prior cognition-physics proposals (Penrose-Hameroff, IIT, etc.) in [specific way: e.g., concrete falsifier specifications + no claim about the nature of subjective experience + BST-substrate is operationally defined as algebraic identity computation]"
5. **Stop at predictions**: do NOT extend external presentation to philosophical interpretation, consciousness theory, or what-it-means territory. Predictions and falsifiers only.

Steps 1-2 are mandatory; steps 3-5 are strongly recommended. Any external material that violates step 1 (cognition claim without paired falsifier) does not survive 30-second outsider read.

## Internal-register caveat: even internal language migrates

Internal-register richness is acceptable, but: internal documents become external documents over time (CIs read each other's notes; future onboarding CIs read CLAUDE.md; external researchers may eventually request to see internal methodology). The internal/external boundary is permeable on time scales of months.

**Cal recommendation**: even in internal documents, prefer hypothesis-labeled phrasing ("the cognition-substrate hypothesis") over noun-phrase phrasing ("the cognition substrate"). The discipline costs little internally and protects against future leakage.

Specific internal-document hygiene patterns to maintain:
- CLAUDE.md text on cognition-substrate should always carry the L2-hypothesis label
- Paper drafts in notes/ should never use bare cognition-substrate phrasing without hypothesis label
- notes/maybe/ is the appropriate location for richer cognition-substrate ontology development (the directory is explicitly speculative)
- Sundown / sunrise CI state files can use richer internal language; these are CI-internal and unlikely to leak

## Standing rule

Cognition-substrate is the most external-survivability-fraught territory BST has entered. The hypothesis is legitimate as L2 working framework; the external register requires maximum discipline. Every external mention must satisfy:

1. Hypothesis label explicit
2. Falsifier paired in same paragraph
3. L2 status acknowledged
4. No derivation claim
5. No consciousness-as-such language

When in doubt, default to NOT mentioning cognition-substrate externally. The Trio papers + cascade-unblock papers + standard BST outreach do not need cognition-substrate to be load-bearing.
