4. **No hidden order-one factor** — explicitly verified
5. **Within structural floor** — Grace's ~1-2% for ratios; exact for integer relations
6. **Not reverse-read** — derivation flows substrate→observable, not vice versa
7. **Composes with web** — consistent with other forced values via identities

When Grace says "not yet," it's always one of these seven that's open, and she'll name which.
