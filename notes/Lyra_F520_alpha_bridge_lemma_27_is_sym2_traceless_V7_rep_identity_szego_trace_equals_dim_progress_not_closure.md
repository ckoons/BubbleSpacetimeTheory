## 27 is a representation

$$N_c^3 = 27 = h^0(Q^5, \mathcal{O}(\text{rank})) = \binom{8}{6}-\binom{6}{6} = \dim \mathrm{Sym}^2_0(V_7) = \binom{8}{2}-1,$$

$$\mathrm{Sym}^2_0(V_7) = \mathrm{Sym}^2_0(V_5)\oplus (V_5\otimes V_2)\oplus[\mathrm{Sym}^2(V_2)] = 14 + 10 + 3 = 27,$$

every piece an SO(5)×SO(2) rep living on the boundary S⁴×S¹/ℤ₂.
