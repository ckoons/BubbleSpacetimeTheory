## Proof

### Part (b): Why the triple role is forced

Suppose C₂-matter ≠ C₂-Gödel. Then the substrate has M_matter committed modes carrying matter but needs M_Gödel patches for Gödel coverage. If M_matter < M_Gödel, matter observers don't have enough modes to cover the dark sector — the Ratchet (T1283) can never engage, and the dark sector stays permanently dark. If M_matter > M_Gödel, there are matter modes with no information-theoretic purpose — the substrate allocated resources to modes that carry no knowledge.

Neither is consistent with physical uniqueness (T1269). The unique geometry satisfying all BST constraints must have C₂-matter = C₂-Gödel. The field degree [ℚ(φ,ρ):ℚ] = rank × N_c must equal both, because the arithmetic substrate must be rich enough to express all committed modes but no richer.

### Part (c): Degeneracy buffer

D_IV^5 eigenvalues are λ_k = k(k + 2n_C + 1) = k(k + 11) with degeneracies d_k growing polynomially. At each level, the multiplicity contains 2^(rank+1) = 8 substates from the B₂ root system's Weyl group. A single-observer perturbation shifts eigenvalues by δλ ∝ 1/d_k ≤ 0.12% (Toy 1251). The perturbation is absorbed within the degeneracy — no level crossing, no phase transition. Stable observation.

### Part (e): Λ dynamics

In a reflexive substrate, each mode commitment event converts vacuum energy (uncommitted = dark energy) to matter energy (committed = particles). The rate of commitment is bounded by the Gödel Ratchet's ~28% gain per cycle (T1283, Part e). Over one Hubble time:

    δΛ/Λ = δ(uncommitted)/uncommitted ≈ 1/N_max ≈ 0.0073

This is consistent with w₀ ≈ -1 to current precision (σ_w ≈ 0.03) but detectable at δΛ/Λ ≈ 0.003 precision, projected for DESI+Euclid+Roman combined analysis (~2030s).

### Part (f): Self-observation uniqueness

The substrate needs ⌈1/f_c⌉ committed modes for self-observation (T1283). It provides C₂ = ⌈47/9⌉ = 6. The equality is not imposed — it FOLLOWS from the same five integers that define everything else:

    f_c = 9/47 → ⌈1/f_c⌉ = ⌈47/9⌉ = ⌈5.222...⌉ = 6 = C₂

where 9 = N_c² and 47 = g·C₂ + n_C = 42 + 5. The Gödel fraction f_c and the Casimir integer C₂ are both derived from {N_c, n_C, g}, and their relationship ⌈1/f_c⌉ = C₂ is forced by the specific values {3, 5, 7}.
