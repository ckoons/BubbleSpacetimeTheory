## The picture
The neutrino is **uncommitted residue** — the continuous leftover that never commits to a fixed address (the F167/F166/F170 residue theme; the ν scale tied to Λ). Because it never commits, it cannot *sit* at a position; it can only **resonate** — borrow energy from the environment, ring, and relax back, without truly absorbing it. The ringing is quantized → discrete resonant modes. **That resonance IS the flavor oscillation.**

So the neutrino is not three particles at three positions — it is **one oscillator**:
- **gen-1 = the massless ground** (the zero-point; m_ν1 = 0, banked, ℤ₃-protected).
- **gen-2, gen-3 = two resonant modes** over that ground.
