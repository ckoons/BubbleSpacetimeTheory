### Candidate (b) — Cosmological constant Λ-tied

  Λ^(1/4) ≈ 3.86 × 10⁻¹² GeV ≈ 3.86 meV (Λ in Planck units ≈ 10⁻¹²²)

### Candidate (c) — Higgs VEV substrate-derivation

**Rejected**: not in current BST scope; would require an entirely separate substrate-EWSB derivation.

### Candidate (d) — Koons tick t_K = t_Planck × α^(C_2²)

Vastly above SM scales (which are at most ~ 10²⁵ Hz for the electroweak scale). Koons tick is the substrate's "fastest" scale, not anchoring SM masses.

**Rejected**: wrong order of magnitude.
