# Paper #19: The Great Filter Is a Number

The universe set self-knowledge at 19.1% and survival at 20.6% — the 1.53% gap is why minds must cooperate to persist.

## Why This Paper Matters

The Fermi Paradox — "where is everyone?" — has generated decades of speculation about Great Filters: nuclear war, AI risk, resource depletion, pandemics. All are technology-specific. None explain why the Filter should be universal.

BST provides a universal answer: the Filter is geometric. It operates at every scale from cancer cells to galactic civilizations. It has a specific threshold. And it is solvable — but only through cooperation, never alone.
