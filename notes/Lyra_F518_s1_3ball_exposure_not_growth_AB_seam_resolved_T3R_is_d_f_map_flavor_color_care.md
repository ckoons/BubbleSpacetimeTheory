# F518 — S¹→3-ball is exposure; the A/B seam is one mechanism; flavor ≠ color (same number)

Three flagged seams, resolved together — they're connected through the Peirce decomposition (F517).

## Resolution 1 — the S¹→3-ball is exposure, not growth

T2511 reads the transverse Peirce space V₁₂ (dim N_c = 3) as **"relabeling, not growth,"** in apparent tension with "the S¹ blows up into a 3-ball" at the ν=0 vertex (F516). The resolution: **V₁₂ is always present** (banked T2511). At the vertex, the SO(2) phase circle S¹ **degenerates** (weight ν→0, the circle shrinks), leaving the residue integral over the always-there transverse V₁₂ 3-ball (volume coefficient 4/3). **Nothing grows** — the vertex **strips** the S¹ and **exposes** the 3-ball that was always there. Casey's "circle blows up into 3D" is precisely *"the circle degenerates, and the always-present 3-ball is exposed."* So the cone-tip −√π closes **without a blow-up**: residue (4√π/3) normalized by the exposed V₁₂ volume (4/3) = √π. The growth-vs-relabeling friction is dissolved: it is exposure, fully consistent with T2511.

## Resolution 2 — the CKM A/B seam closes by identification

- **Picture A** (F191): up = down shifted by the T₃ᴿ automorphism.
- **Picture B** (F381/F413): up and down at the *same* radii, differing only by the coupling function (down = bulk d(ν), up = boundary f(ν)).

These are **not competing mechanisms** — they are the same up/down difference in two languages. The banked mass sector already fixes it: down = **bulk** ladder d(ν) (F506 {1,3,5}), up = **boundary** shells f(ν) (F507/F508). So up and down differ by the **bulk-vs-boundary coupling function** — that *is* Picture B. And the **T₃ᴿ automorphism** (Picture A) is exactly what distinguishes up (T₃ᴿ=+½) from down (−½) — **realized as the bulk-d(ν) ↔ boundary-f(ν) coupling change.** So T₃ᴿ (group-theoretic, A) = the d↔f coupling map (geometric, B). The seam closes, consistent with the banked bulk/boundary split. **The concrete CKM blocker is now singular and sharp: derive f(ν) = T₃ᴿ(d(ν))** — the up boundary coupling as the T₃ᴿ image of the down bulk coupling.

## Resolution 3 — flavor-vs-color care (Keeper's flag)

The CKM mixing weight N_c is the **counting** weight (the Trichotomy: mass=measuring=2n_C, mixing=counting=N_c), which numerically equals dim V₁₂ (the **color** Peirce space) = 3 — but CKM is **flavor** mixing, not color. **Same number N_c=3, distinct roles:** flavor-counting (Trichotomy) vs color-space (V₁₂). Do **not** assert that the color space V₁₂ *drives* flavor mixing (Elie's Peirce-color candidate needs this care) — the N_c mixing-weight is the counting weight; its equality with dim V₁₂ is the **a = N_c over-determination** (a = n_C − rank = N_c), the same coincidence that makes the cone-tip 3-ball "the three commitments." Note it, don't over-identify it.
