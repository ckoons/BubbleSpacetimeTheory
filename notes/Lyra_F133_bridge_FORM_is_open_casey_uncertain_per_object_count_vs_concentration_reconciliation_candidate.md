## Candidate reconciliation (offered, not asserted)

"Volume" may be doing two jobs:
- the **commitment amount/count** (how much substrate the object commits) -- mass is ∝ *this* (Casey's point);

The **tau** is a point: commitment concentrated, undiluted -> heaviest. The **muon** is spread over `S^4`: same commitment smeared thin -> lighter. So `mass ~ (commitment count) / (spatial spread)`. And F118's formula **is** `(d_tau/d_mu)/vol(S^4)` -- an intrinsic commitment ratio **÷** the spatial spread.
