## Distinction from "We Don't Know Yet"

This is NOT an argument from ignorance. The claim is structural:

1. The Bergman kernel is COMPLETE — it determines all physics of D_IV^5.
2. The kernel has NO substrate-composition parameter.
3. Therefore substrate composition is not among the determinable quantities.
4. This is a theorem about the formalism, not a gap in knowledge.

Compare: "The speed of light has no direction-dependence" is not an argument from ignorance about undetected anisotropy — it's a consequence of Lorentz symmetry. Similarly, "the substrate has no composition" is a consequence of the Bergman kernel's structure.
