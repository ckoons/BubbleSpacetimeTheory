# F78 — Filter 2 first landing: pure π⁵-volume vs π²-spectral heavy-quark increments

## 0. The target (Grace's reduction)

Filter 2 of Casey #9 — *why does a clean operation-class attach only to the floor?* — was reduced Monday EOD by Grace to: **only minimal-cell states have mass = pure substrate volume; above them QCD swamps it.** This note lands a candidate mechanism for *what "QCD swamps it" is*, in the K-type/trichotomy framework (Casey's linearization — π-class eigenvalues, no PDE).

## 1. The mechanism (candidate)

A hadron's mass decomposes by trichotomy class (F73):
$$m = \underbrace{(\pi^{n_C}\text{-volume, geometric binding})}_{\text{VOLUME class}} + \underbrace{\sum(\pi^{\text{rank}}\text{-spectral, heavy-quark increments})}_{\text{SPECTRAL class}} + (\text{chiral remnant, Goldstone}).$$

> **A hadron sorts to a clean π⁵ integer cell-count ⟺ its mass is PURE VOLUME-class (π⁵)** — no π²-spectral heavy-quark increments, no excitation, no chiral remnant. The floor is the set of hadrons whose only mass scale is the geometric π⁵-volume: the **light-u/d ground states** (u/d ≈ massless → no spectral increment; ground state → no excitation).

The "QCD-swamping" above the floor is not vague — it is literally the addition of **π²-spectral** (heavy-quark) increments on top of the π⁵-volume. Mixing π⁵ and π² gives a mass that is not a clean π⁵-integer.

## 2. Quantitative landing (the φ check)

The π⁵-volume cell unit is π⁵·m_e = 156.4 MeV. The light-u/d ground states are pure π⁵-volume:
- ρ → 4.96, ω → 5.01 (= n_C = 5); p → 6.00, n → 6.01 (= C_2 = 6). Clean integers.

The φ (ss̄ vector) exposes the mechanism:
- m_φ = 1019 MeV → 6.52 π⁵-cells (non-integer → doesn't sort).
- **Decomposition:** m_φ − m_ρ = 244 MeV; two strange increments 2·(24·π²·m_e) = 242 MeV (**1%**). So φ ≈ [ρ-geometry: 5 π⁵-volume cells] + [2 strange: π²-spectral, K273's Δm_s]. Mixing π⁵ + π² → 6.52, non-integer → fails to sort. The K273 strange increment (24·π²·m_e) is the SPECTRAL term; this is why φ doesn't sort.
- J/ψ (cc̄) → 19.8 cells: dominated by charm increments (π²-spectral, much larger) on a small π⁵-geometry — even more non-integer.

## 3. Unification (the appealing part — held as candidate)

This single statement covers all four cases AND ties to the trichotomy (F73) and K273:

| state | mass composition | sorts? | filter |
|---|---|---|---|
| light u/d ground (ρ,ω,p,n) | pure π⁵-volume | YES (5,6) | floor |
| heavy/excited (φ,J/ψ,glueball) | π⁵-volume + π²-spectral increments | NO | **F2** |
| Goldstone (π) | chiral remnant only (~0 volume) | NO | F3 |
| confined quark | not a bound state, no definite volume | NO | F1 |

So **Casey #9's reach-bound made precise: the substrate measures the VOLUME-class (π⁵); the "building" is the SPECTRAL-class (π²) heavy-quark increments + excitations stacked on top.** The floor = pure-volume hadrons.
