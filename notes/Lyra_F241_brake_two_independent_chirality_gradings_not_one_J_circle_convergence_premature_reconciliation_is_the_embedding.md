| SO(2) sign | SO(4) type |
|---|---|
| +½ | (2,1) |
| +½ | (1,2) |
| −½ | (2,1) |
| −½ | (1,2) |

A fixed-charge spinor still contains **both** Weyl pieces — so the **SO(2) sign and the SO(4) (2,1)/(1,2) type are independent quantum numbers.** They do not align automatically. Hence two distinct chirality-like gradings:
