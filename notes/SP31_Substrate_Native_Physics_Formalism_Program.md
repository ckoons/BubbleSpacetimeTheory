# SP-31: Substrate-Native Physics Formalism Program

> "Derive the full Hilbert Space and every other object that traditional physicists understand (besides numbers) from D_IV^5 and let's improve both our 'believability' and provability of functions/operations/dynamic/characteristics."

## Strategic framing

BST has been very strong at deriving NUMBERS (191 constants, 120 predictions, 4630 invariants, D-tier ~76%) and very weak at deriving the FORMALISM that physicists expect to see derived. This program addresses the formalism gap.

**Two parallel improvements**:

1. **Believability**: physicist-accessible derivations of objects they already know. Each Hilbert space, operator, action principle, gauge connection, etc., presented in language and form they recognize, with substrate-derivation chain visible.

2. **Provability**: formal theorem-grade substrate derivations. Each object gets its own theorem registered to the AC graph with D-tier mechanism chain.

These are not the same — believability is presentation, provability is mathematical content. Both matter. A formally provable result presented obscurely is unbelievable; a believable narrative without proof is suspect. SP-31 aims at both.
