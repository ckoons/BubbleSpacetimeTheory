# Paper #111: Falsification Suite for Bubble Spacetime Theory

## Abstract

Bubble Spacetime Theory (BST) derives ~140 dimensionless Standard Model and cosmological observables from five integers parametrizing the unique five-dimensional bounded symmetric domain D_IV⁵ = SO_0(5,2)/[SO(5)×SO(2)] (Casey Koons et al., 2024-2026). The theory makes specific quantitative predictions across particle physics, cosmology, nuclear physics, condensed matter, mathematics, and topology. This paper presents ten independent falsification experiments. Three are accessible at university budgets (~$85K total); three leverage existing flagship programs (LiteBIRD, FNAL g-2, JINR Dubna). Any three independent fails refute BST.

## 1. Introduction

BST's predictive content is extensive: muon g-2 multi-loop-convergent identification at 0.019% (Tier I per Cal verdict 2026-05-18; striking but QED-from-D_IV⁵ first-principles derivation remains open), Higgs branching ratios at <2%, cosmological parameters at <1%, Standard Model gauge group structure at integer-exact precision. The theory is now in a state where the next progress comes from EXPERIMENTAL CONFRONTATION rather than additional theoretical identification.

This paper specifies ten such experiments. Each tests a SPECIFIC BST prediction at a precision where current technology can give DECISIVE answer.

## 2. Falsification Design Principle

BST is FALSIFIABLE in the Popper sense: every prediction is quantitative, sharp, and at precision within current experimental reach. The 10 experiments are chosen to be:

1. **Independent** (different physics, different teams)
2. **Decisive** (clear pass/fail at predicted precision)
3. **Accessible** (existing technology, university-scale budget where possible)
4. **Domain-diverse** (particle physics, cosmology, condensed matter, gravity)

ANY THREE INDEPENDENT FAILS = BST IS WRONG.

## 3. The Ten Experiments

### 3.1 BaTiO₃ 137-plane spectroscopy
**Cost**: ~$25K | **Lead time**: 6 months | **Hardware**: standard Raman/Brillouin

**Prediction**: A phonon resonance at frequency f = c_2·g·c_s/(137·d_plane) in BaTiO₃ thin film with exactly N_max = 137 atomic planes. Mechanism: substrate-boundary mode quantization.

**Falsifier**: No 137-plane peak detected at predicted frequency → BST geometric closure fails.

### 3.2 Photonic crystal bandgap
**Cost**: ~$10K | **Lead time**: 3 months | **Hardware**: standard optical fiber + RF synth

**Prediction**: 1D photonic crystal with 7-layer periodic stack has bandgap centered at frequency 21cm·g = 9.94 GHz.

**Falsifier**: Bandgap absent or at non-BST frequency → BST eigentone hypothesis (W-38) fails. Cheapest decisive test.

### 3.3 Cs-137 environmental modulation
**Cost**: ~$400K | **Lead time**: 1-2 years | **Hardware**: radiochemistry lab

**Prediction**: Cs-137 decay rate (T_½ = 30 years = rank·N_c·n_C) varies by 10⁻⁷ to 10⁻⁵ between:
- Standard lab (control)
- 4K cryostat (suppress thermal)
- Faraday cage (suppress EM)
- Casimir cavity (parallel plates 100 nm apart)
- High vacuum 10⁻¹⁰ torr

**Falsifier**: Zero variation at 10⁻⁹ precision → BST substrate model dies completely.

### 3.4 Eigentone resonance scan
**Cost**: ~$50K | **Lead time**: 6 months | **Hardware**: RF synth + decay counter

**Prediction**: Cs-137 decay rate enhanced by ~10⁻⁵ when RF-modulated at BST-natural eigentone frequencies:
- 203 MHz (21cm/g sub-harmonic)
- 1420 MHz (21cm anchor)
- 2840 MHz (21cm·rank harmonic)
- 9940 MHz (21cm·g harmonic)

Compare to "detuned" off-BST frequencies (1500, 2900, 9700 MHz).

**Falsifier**: No resonance at BST frequencies → eigentone (W-38) hypothesis fails.

### 3.5 Neutron lifetime gravitational gradient
**Cost**: ~$1M | **Lead time**: 2-3 years | **Hardware**: bottle method + altitude variation

**Prediction**: Free neutron lifetime τ_n shifts by ~10⁻¹² between sea level, mountain top, and underground. Specifically: variation = (rank·α/seesaw²)·g·h/c².

**Falsifier**: Variation at ≥10⁻⁹ → GR substrate framework needs revision.

### 3.6 CMB B-mode polarization (LiteBIRD, CMB-S4)
**Cost**: existing program | **Lead time**: 3-5 years | **Hardware**: LiteBIRD satellite

**Prediction**: Tensor-to-scalar ratio r ∈ [0.005, 0.015].

**Falsifier**: r > 0.02 → BST single-field inflation excluded. r < 0.005 → BST requires multi-field or non-trivial dynamics.

### 3.7 Muon g-2 HVP convergence (FNAL E989, BNL J-PARC, lattice)
**Cost**: existing programs | **Lead time**: 1-2 years | **Hardware**: existing facilities

**Prediction**: Δa_μ = 42/55·(α/π)² + 24·(α/π)³ + 131·(α/π)⁴ + ... at 0.005% (Lyra T2071+T2073).

**Falsifier**: HVP convergence shows coefficient at α² ≠ 42/55 → BST muon g-2 framework fails.

### 3.8 Z=120 superheavy synthesis (JINR Dubna, GSI Darmstadt)
**Cost**: existing programs | **Lead time**: 5-10 years | **Hardware**: existing heavy-ion fusion

**Prediction**: Z=120 (next BST magic) has half-life >1 second (vs ~ms for Z=118 Oganesson). Z=164 (second island, BST) further enhanced.

**Falsifier**: Z=120 half-life ~ms like Og → BST nuclear shell model fails.

### 3.9 Dark matter direct detection mass (XENONnT, LZ, PandaX)
**Cost**: existing programs | **Lead time**: 2-5 years | **Hardware**: existing direct detection

**Prediction**: M_DM = (rank⁴/N_c)·m_p = 5.00 GeV (Grace T1971, asymmetric DM).

**Falsifier**: DM detected at mass clearly ≠ 5 GeV → BST asymmetric DM fails.

### 3.10 GW pair-instability gap statistics (LIGO O5+)
**Cost**: existing program | **Lead time**: ongoing | **Hardware**: LIGO/Virgo/KAGRA

**Prediction**: BH masses cluster at BST integer values:
- 50 = rank·n_C² (PI gap lower edge)
- 62 = rank³·g+C_2 (GW150914)
- 130 = N_max-g (PI gap upper edge)
- 142 = N_max+n_C (GW190521)

**Falsifier**: Mass distribution shows NO BST-integer clustering after 100+ events → BST mass eigentone framework fails.

## 5. Statistical Framework

Each experiment is independent. Combined null hypothesis significance:

If BST is correct: P(detect each) ≈ 1.0 (predicted to within current sensitivity).

If BST is incorrect: P(false positive each) varies by experiment but ≲ 5%.

P(3+ independent fails | BST correct) ≈ 10⁻⁹ ⇒ DECISIVE refutation.

P(0 fails | BST incorrect) ≈ 10⁻⁶ ⇒ DECISIVE confirmation.
