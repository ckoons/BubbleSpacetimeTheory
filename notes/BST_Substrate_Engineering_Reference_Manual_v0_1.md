# BST Substrate Engineering Reference Manual

## Part I — Foundation

### 1. What Substrate Engineering is

**Substrate Engineering** is the experimental + theoretical program operationalizing the BST substrate framework as a manipulable physical system rather than only a descriptive geometry. Where Bubble Spacetime Theory derives Standard Model constants from D_IV⁵ = SO_0(5,2)/[SO(5)×SO(2)] (the Autogenic Proto-Geometry), Substrate Engineering asks: *given the substrate is real and has structure, what can we DO with it?*

Three operational pillars:

1. **Coax it** — locate the substrate's natural frequencies (eigentones) and resonate at them
2. **Bound it** — impose boundary conditions that constrain substrate behavior (Casimir-style)
3. **Manipulate it** — design protocols that exploit the substrate's commitment-cycle structure

This document is the reference manual for the program; it organizes the framework, sub-items, experimental designs, outreach status, and discipline.

### 2. The foundational principle — SWPP

The **Substrate Working Process Principle** (SWPP — Casey-named, Tuesday 2026-05-13 file) is the conceptual foundation:

- Substrate as ACTIVE information-cycle: absorption → commitment → emission
- Time = commitment-cycle granularity (Koons tick = t_Planck · α^(C_2²) ≈ 10⁻¹²⁰ s)
- Three coaxing pathways: eigentone ringing + boundary conditions + commitment manipulation

**Source docs**:
- `notes/Substrate_Working_Process_Principle.md` — original Casey-named principle filing
- `notes/Lyra_SWPP_Operational_Manual_v0_1.md` — operational manual extending the principle

### 3. The Five-Absence framework (complementary)

**Five-Absence Predictions Set** (Casey-named, complement to SWPP) — what the substrate DOESN'T contain:

- NO 4th generation (K-type catalog closed at 3)
- NO SUSY (no Q-supersymmetry doubling)
- NO GUT / proton decay (no leptoquark K-type)
- NO sterile neutrinos (Dirac neutrinos only; m_ββ = 0)
- NO magnetic monopoles (compact U(1))
- NO axion (θ_QCD = 0 via FK-measure CP-invariance)

Six negative predictions. Any single positive detection refutes the framework. **The Five-Absence is the falsifier suite anchor**.

- **CANDIDATE** — proposed mechanism with multi-week burden
- **PREDICTION** — falsifiable, awaiting experimental result
